# AI Radar Demo Trend Analysis

## Last 7 days

- Frontier global labs are clustering around agent workflow control, lower-latency coding assistance, and multimodal task execution.
- China signals are concentrated in official model releases and assistant capability packaging, especially where products are framed for broader developer or enterprise adoption.
- GitHub and Hacker News confirm that developer attention is tracking agent tooling and observability, not just headline model launches.

## Last 30 days

- Agent-oriented product design remains the clearest product direction across Silicon Valley sources.
- Enterprise packaging is now a recurring layer on top of model releases, APIs, and multimodal features.
- Open source and developer ecosystem signals amplify the importance of official launches when SDKs or repo releases follow quickly.

## China vs Global

Global signals show broader variety across agents, APIs, SDK releases, and ecosystem heat. China signals are fewer but remain highly first-hand because they come directly from official product surfaces, especially around model releases and assistant upgrades.
